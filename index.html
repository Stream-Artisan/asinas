Welcome

Welcome to All Saints Institute of Nursing and Allied Sciences, a premier institution dedicated to shaping the future of healthcare through unparalleled nursing education.

Our globally recognized curriculum blends cutting-edge academic instruction with immersive clinical experience, tailored to meet both international standards and local healthcare needs.

At All Saints, nursing is a calling. Join our vibrant community and make a lasting impact in healthcare.

Admissions

We seek passionate candidates with academic excellence and a commitment to nursing, ready to contribute to the healthcare community.

Generic BSN (4 years) Degree Program

  • Matric Science (Physics, Chemistry & Biology)
  • FSC (Pre-Medical) with 50% marks minimum
  • Age Limit: 14-35 years
  • Gender Ratio: Open merit

POST RN-BSN (2 Years) Degree Program

  • Matric with Science or Arts (per HEC)
  • 03-Year Diploma in General Nursing
  • 01-Year Diploma in Midwifery
  • 01 Year as a Registered Nurse
  • Gender: Both
