POST RN-BSN (2 Years)

Advancing Nursing Careers with Leadership and Expertise

Program Overview

This is a POST RN-BSN (2 Years) Degree Program, leading to practice as a Registered Nurse. The degree program fosters integrated nursing knowledge, skills, and professional attitudes. Upon completion of this program, the graduate is prepared to be a safe clinical nurse, who will be able to provide comprehensive care at primary, secondary, and tertiary levels within hospitals and community settings.

Moreover, the Post RN students will be registered with Pakistan Nursing & Midwifery Council after completing one year internship as per given PNC internship policy.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Matric with Science (Biology, Chemistry & Physics as compulsory subjects) OR Matric Arts per HEC Notification No.8 (61)/A&A/2018/HEC/5998 dt.06.01.2018.
  • 03-Year Diploma in General Nursing
  • 1-Year Diploma in Midwifery
  • 01-Year Diploma in Midwifery (any post-basic specialty for males in lieu of midwifery)
  • Gender: Both

Experience

  • 01 Year as a Registered Nurse

Why Choose This Program?

Tailored specifically for working professionals, the POST RN-BSN (2 Years) Degree Program provides a flexible, accommodating schedule designed to seamlessly integrate with your busy lifestyle. This comprehensive curriculum emphasizes advanced nursing practices, cutting-edge research methodologies, and robust leadership development, empowering you to excel in high-impact roles within the healthcare industry. Through a blend of theoretical coursework and hands-on clinical experiences, the program equips you with the skills to navigate complex healthcare challenges, drive innovation, and lead multidisciplinary teams. With a focus on evidence-based practice and strategic decision-making, this degree prepares you for influential positions such as clinical nurse leader, healthcare administrator, or advanced practice nurse, fostering both personal growth and professional excellence in dynamic healthcare environments.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the POST RN-BSN (2 Years) Degree Program is exceptionally well-prepared to take on dynamic leadership roles in the nursing profession. Career opportunities include positions such as Nurse Manager, Clinical Educator, and Healthcare Administrator, where they can lead teams, mentor fellow professionals, and influence patient care outcomes.

In addition to immediate employment prospects, our graduates are also well-positioned for further academic advancement. Many pursue postgraduate studies in nursing, healthcare administration, or public health, opening doors to specialized roles and higher-level responsibilities within the healthcare sector. This program lays a strong foundation for those aiming to make a lasting impact through nursing leadership, health systems innovation, and evidence-based practice.
