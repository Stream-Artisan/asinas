POST RN-BSN (2 Years)

Program Overview

The POST RN-BSN program is a 2-year degree designed for registered nurses who wish to advance their careers. This program builds on existing nursing knowledge and skills, providing a pathway to leadership roles in healthcare.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Matric with Science or Arts (per HEC)
  • 03-Year Diploma in General Nursing
  • 01-Year Diploma in Midwifery
  • 01 Year as a Registered Nurse
  • Gender: Both

Why Choose This Program?

This program is tailored for working professionals, offering flexible schedules and a curriculum focused on advanced nursing practices, research, and leadership development.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the POST RN-BSN program can pursue leadership roles in healthcare, including nurse manager, clinical educator, and healthcare administrator. They are also eligible for advanced studies in nursing and healthcare management.
