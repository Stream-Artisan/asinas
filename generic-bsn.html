Generic BSN (4 years)

Program Overview

The Generic BSN program is a comprehensive 4-year degree designed to prepare students for a rewarding career in nursing. This program combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on clinical experience to ensure graduates are well-equipped to meet the challenges of modern healthcare.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Matric Science (Physics, Chemistry & Biology)
  • FSC (Pre-Medical) with 50% marks minimum
  • Age Limit: 14-35 years
  • Gender Ratio: Open merit

Why Choose This Program?

This program offers a unique blend of academic excellence and practical training. Students will have access to state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and opportunities for clinical rotations in top hospitals.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the Generic BSN program can pursue careers in various healthcare settings, including hospitals, clinics, community health centers, and research institutions. They are also eligible to pursue advanced degrees in nursing and healthcare management.
